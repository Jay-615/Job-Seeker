# Job Search Criteria

*Example persona: Sam Chen, 8 years e-commerce product management, based in
Seattle. Use this file as a model for your own `criteria.md`.*

## Roles (job titles to search for)
- Senior Product Manager
- Product Manager
- Principal Product Manager
- Lead Product Manager

## Locations
- Seattle metro (within 30 miles of Seattle, WA)
- Remote (US-based)
- Hybrid with Seattle HQ
- Hybrid with Bellevue HQ

## Seniority
- Accept: Senior IC (Senior PM, Lead PM, Principal PM), mid-level PM (will be scored lower than senior target)
- Reject: VP+, Head of, Chief, Director (above target level), Associate PM, Junior PM, Intern

## Compensation floor
- $140,000 annual OR $80/hour (only applied if comp is disclosed in posting)

## Job types
- Full-time
- Contract
- Contract-to-hire

## Industries (used as soft signal during scoring)

For Senior PM and PM roles:
- E-commerce (marketplace, DTC, retail platforms)
- Tech (SaaS, developer tools, consumer apps)
- Fintech (payments, banking, lending, personal finance)

Adjacent industries treated as partial match:
- Logistics and fulfillment
- Subscription commerce
- Adtech tied to commerce platforms
